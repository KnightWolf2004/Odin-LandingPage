Batman's Top Gadgets
Batman is famous for his aresenal of cool tech. These are 4 for his most used gadgets that we have seen over the years.
Batarang
Batarangs are bat-shaped throwing weapons used by Batman. These are the most basic and commonly used items in his arsenal.
Grappling Gun
Grapple Guns are hi-tech pistols that fire small Grappling Hooks fitted on high-tensile wire.
Batclaw
The Batclaw was an attachment for Batman's grapnel gun. The Batclaw was often used as both a combat tool or create maneuverability options.
Utility Belt
The Utility Belt is the iconic storage device used to carry most of Bruce Wayne's gadgets and equipment over his Batsuit.
